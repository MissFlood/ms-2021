Who We Are

Metal Solutions Ltd are Multi-Metal and Multi-System distributors to the traditional roofing and cladding industry. However we are far more than distributors. Due to our many years of technical and contracting experience, we are able to offer specific advice on all Metals and Systems. Our Metals

Impartial advice

All Manufacturers will tell you that their particular metal is the best, however the best Metal is the one that satisfies the Client’s needs the most. Metal Solutions Ltd can offer impartial advice on all metals, whether it be Aluminium, Brass, Bronze, Copper, Stainless Steel, Steel, Zinc or other alloys.

Guidance from our experts

We can assist you through the metal minefield and explain the nuances of each material, whether it be the colour choices, workability, weathering process, what to expect as the metal ages, ultimate life span, as well as the all-important eco-credentials.

Order direct from stock

All metals are processed in-house from our large stocks of master coils, so all combinations of coil width and length, as well as sheets are available to order.

We're here to help

If you already know which Metal you require then please have a look at what we offer, we are still here to help. All our Metal and System are available for specification via the NBS (National Building Specification).

Our Rainwater Systems

Metal Solutions Ltd manufacture rainwater goods in Zinc, Copper, Stainless Steel and other alloys, as well as supplying our branded GreenCoat GreenLine® colour coated galvanised steel system.

Zinc Rainwater System

Natural, light preweathered & dark preweathered zinc gutters, pipes and accessories, giving a cost-effective yet pleasing visual experience.

Stainless Steel Rainwater System

Classic brush rolled 316 grade stainless steel gutters, pipe and accessories, infinitely compatible with all other building materials and very long lasting.

Copper Rainwater System

Natural copper gutters, pipes and accessories that age over time from a bright initial finidh, through brown and eventually forming the beautiful blue green patina.

GreenCoat® Rainwater System

Colour coated gutters, pipes and accessories made from SSAB GreenCoat® RWS coated steel, readily available and easy to install.
